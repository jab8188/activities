Indoor Activities

  • Painting what your imagination desires
  • Competitions such as stacking cups or drawing the fastest animal
  • Building with wood, lego, cardboard boxes
  • Cards or Board games with the whole family

Outdoor Activities

  • Races from point A to point B or try it with a challange such as try carrying an egg on a spoon or fork, see who makes it without dropping or breaking their egg
  • Start a garden, can start small and expand as you go. Can try just flowers or growing your own food, herbs, and tea
  • Build with sand, mud, rocks; let your mind get creative and get your hands a little dirty

More Ideas

  • Splatter painting on canvas
  • Take a hike in a local area near you or take a day trip to a new park and find trails
